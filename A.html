Island in the Aegean Sea

Country: Greece
Administrative region:South Aegean
Regional unit: Thira
Santorini is one of the Cyclades islands in the Aegean Sea.
It was devastated by a volcanic eruption in the 16th century BC, forever shaping its rugged landscape.
The rare natural beauty of Santorini, the breathtaking caldera, the views, the extraordinary volcanic beaches and the lunar landscapes.
